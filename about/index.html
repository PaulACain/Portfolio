Hello hello,

I’m a coffee fanatic turned designer based in the greater Philadelphia area. My career began as a hands on consultant for cafés. During this period I became heavily involded with brand experience and fell in love with the work. My background as leader and team player has laid the bedrock for my approach to design thinking. I try to follow one rule: trust your instincts, and always test your theories.

Throughout my journey, I’ve learned a lot about what it means to be a designer. Since my first “ahhh - yep this is what I want to do” moment, I’ve created a number of posters, logos, stickers, pin buttons, and even designed product labels for coffee bags. One of the most important things I’ve learned throughout this process is that design can have a lasting impact on people. And that’s both humbled me and scared the living sh*t out of me. The idea that something I’ve done can affect people's emotions, outlook, or even opinions has become addicting. I want to use design in all shapes and forms to affect people’s lives.
