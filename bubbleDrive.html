Understanding the lay of the land

Competitive analysis

In order to understand where an opportunity might lie in the cloud storage space, I examined the major players in the landscape. What I became acutely aware of is how the market demands niche storage locations catering to a unique types of user. These specialized digital storage spaces are curated to specific people, industries, and even products.

With that in mind, I suggested to the client to leverage their position and create an application that caters to entrepreneurs and small businesses. You can find my whole competitive analysis report here.

User research

In order to learn more about potential users for this application, I created a user research survey. In this process I was attempting to learn following:

  • How familiar are people with cloud storage applications
  • What application features are most important to users
  • Is there is a market for a cloud storage tool in the small businesses space

    • After gathering 17 responses from individuals across the world from with a diverse background, I was able to determine the following from the surveys:

  • Users were extremely familiar with cloud storage applications with approximately nearly 50% using a product for work
  • The most important features for applications are ability to upload content, the ability to share and the ability to create content all within the application.
  • Of my 17 participants, 76% have worked in some capacity in a small business. Of those, 84% said that a cloud storage tool that allows owners/managers to share and collaborate with staff would be beneficial
